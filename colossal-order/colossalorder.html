About us

Colossal Order is an independent Finnish-based game development studio and the creators of the critically acclaimed city-builder Cities: Skylines, and the popular mass transit simulation games Cities in Motion and Cities in Motion 2.

We are deeply passionate about simulation games and with over 10 years of experience in the field, we create the world's best simulation games from our home in Tampere, Finland. Our team consists of 30 people from 10 different countries who work from home or our modern central office, and we enjoy all the benefits of Nordic work culture.

We have a flexible and friendly work environment, open communication, and a healthy work-life balance. We believe that happy and healthy employees are the key to success. It's no coincidence we work in the happiest country in the world!
